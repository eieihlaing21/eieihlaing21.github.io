Mark Lee is a Korean-Canadian artist under SM Entertainment . He is best known for being the main rapper and vocalist of the boy group NCT and its sub-units NCT U,NCT 127, NCT Dream,and Super M.

Born in Canada, Mark was able to audition for SM Entertainment through the 'SM Global Audition' in Vancouver and became a trainee of the agency for four years before finally debuting as a member of NCT.

On August 24, 2016, Mark debuted as a member of the sub-unit NCT Dream. However, in December 2018, Mark graduated from the group due to its rotational system based on age.

In 2022, Mark eventually began to release his solo songs through SM Station's NCT Lab, such as "Child" and "Golden Hour" .

  127 Concert Tour

Neo City-The Link was the second concert tour headlined by NCT 127, in support of their third studio album Sticker(2021)and fourth studio album 2 Baddies(2022).

The tour run from December 2021 until January 2023, with a total of 28 shows in 17 cities across Asia, North America, and Latin America.

It recorded over 700,000 spectators from stadiums and arenas, making it NCT 127's biggest tour yet.It was the group's first in-person concert tour since Neo City- The Origin, two years prior, following the cancellation of Neo City- The Awards in 2020 due to the COVID-19 pandemic.

  Dream Concert Tour

The Dream Show 2: In A Dream was the first worldwide concert tour and second tour overall headlined by NCT Dream, in support of their second studio album Glitch Mode(2022).

The tour kicked off in September 2022 and concluded in July 2023,it was the group's first in-person concert tour in three years.
