Family & Personal Life

Mark Lee was born on August 2, 1999 in Toronto, Ontario, Canada to Korean parents.He has an three years older brother. He live in Canada util he was six years old, when his family moved to South Korea.He attended Eonju Middle School. He graduated on February 7, 2018.He also joined a music school and later, entrolled in the 'Seoul School of Performing Arts'.He speaks fluent Korean and English.


Career

Mark Lee started his musicial journey when his parents entrolled him in a music school after moving to South Korea from Canada. In 2012, he decided to enter a competition called 'S.M. Global Audition'. December 2013, he had become part of a group of trainees 'S.M. Rookies'. On April 4, 2016, S.M. Entertainment announced that Mark Lee would make his debut as a member of the boy group 'NCT U'.In July 2016, Mark Lee was selected as a member of the second sub-unit, NCT 127.In August 2016, Mark Lee made history by getting selected as a member of the third sub-unit 'NCT DREAM'.South Korean music television channel 'Ments' selected him to be part of their hip-hop show, High School Rapper, in January 2017. He became the co-host of 'MBC' television channel's 'Music Core' show.Mark Lee is also a talented songwriter and has written lyrics for some of NCT's songs.Since he spoke English very well, he became an unofficial leader of the group when interacting with international fans.He is known for his rap skills and charismatic stage presence.Mark is a talented and versatile artist who has achieved a lot in his career.He is still pursuing his passion and dreams in the music industry.He is admired by many fans and peers for his hard work and dedication.
