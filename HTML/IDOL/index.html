Early life and education

Zhou Yi Ran, born in Chongqing, is a Chinese singer and actor.He made his debut in 2017 as a member of Yi'an Music Club, a cross-dimensional music group.However, in 2018, he announced that he would be leaving the group. He never revealed more information about his mother, father, brother, and sister.He is not married yet. He is not dating anyone in 2023 and has not officially shared any data about his relationship status.According to some netizens, in 2021 he was in a relationship with actress Dai Xinyi. He has a huge fan following and his fans are very curious to know about his current girlfriend's name.
